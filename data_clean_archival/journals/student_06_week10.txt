For my final project, I supported the contracts and proposal pricing team in developing a GSA schedule. I researched updated federal pricing for various technical roles, including project officers, budgeting officers, and monitoring and evaluation officers, and compiled an Excel spreadsheet outlining role classifications, SIN numbers, and hourly pricing ranges. I also analyzed other organizations holding GSA schedules, reviewing their terms, conditions, and pricing structures, and organized this information into a comparative spreadsheet.

A challenge I encountered was navigating the GSA website, as it was unfamiliar to me. I addressed this by maintaining focus, conducting independent research, and regularly consulting my supervisor for clarification. Through persistence and structured inquiry, I was able to complete the assignment successfully.
